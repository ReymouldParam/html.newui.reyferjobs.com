In this digital and millennial dominated era, it is easy to know information of job vacancy in your dream company. On the flipside, it is equally hard to get an interview for the same role. When a candidate applies directly through job portals or through company website, it is very usual for a Job seeker to feel ghosted by the recruiters. The candidate does not have insights whether the job application has reached the HR team or the Hiring Manager.

To overcome the issue, the best option for the candidate is to reach the HR through existing employee in an organization in the form of EMPLOYEE REFERRAL PROGRAM. Employee referrals will not assure the job interview but the candidate is assured that his job application reached and reviewed by the HR of that organization. Employee referrals will assure a candidate chances of getting hired are more by 7%.

Organizations have always been encouraging its employees to refer candidates as the cost per hire is low and rate of retention of is high in this program. Companies even incentivize employees to refer candidates by offering a monetary reward if their referral is hired. It is always an opportunity for the employees to help company by finding right talent and thereby earn passive income.

But there is always a gap between the Job seeker and referrer. Our vision is to bridge this gap and create a win, win situation for both Job seeker and referrer.

The whole recruitment process is cumbersome process with filtering a lot of resumes based on skills and job requirements. To make this process easier for the Hiring Managers and job referrers we have developed an ATS (Application Tracking System)/ Intelligence algorithm to rank the candidates based on match between skills of the Job seeker and Job role requirements and various other factors. This makes the process easy for Job poster/ Referrer to filter the suitable candidate in the applications received and refer him to the Job.
